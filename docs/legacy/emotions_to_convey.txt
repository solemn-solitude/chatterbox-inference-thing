## Notes on Coverage:
- Removed obvious synonyms (e.g., enraged/jubilant/exasperated)
- Added missing basic emotions: disgust, contempt, shame, guilt, envy, boredom
- Included NSFW/intimate states: lustful, yearning (important for romantic contexts)
- Added energy-level states: energized, exhausted, sleepy, restless
- Included social dynamics: defensive, condescending, sarcastic, cynical
- Added power dynamics: defiant, protective, assertive, insecure
- Each emotion should produce DISTINCT prosodic signatures
- Focus on conversational/companion AI contexts, not theatrical extremes
